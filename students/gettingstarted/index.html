Student - Getting Started

My Courses

To access the Kerboodle website you will need to have received your login details from your teacher or school administrator. These details will consist of a username, a password and an institution code. You might have also received a Group Code from your teacher, which is used to access a particular course. To find out more information please read the group code section below.

If you have misplaced or forgotten any of your details then please speak to your teacher. Oxford University Press are unable to give out details to students.

Logging in

When you first login to the website you will need to change your password, it is also advised that you add an email address. If you have an email address registered on your account, you will be able to reset your password if you need to in the future. Please click on the Save Changes button after you have made any changes to your details.

Group Codes

If your teacher hasn’t already assigned you to a course then you will see the screen pictured below once you have logged in. In this scenario your teacher should have given you a Group Code to access the course. Please click on the green Enter Group Code button on the right hand side of the screen and enter the code. If you don’t have your Group Code for your course then please contact your teacher.

Homepage

Once you have confirmed your details, you should then see the course on your Home screen. All the courses you have access to will be shown in tiles in the centre of your screen and in the My Course dropdown. Click on a course tile to gain access to all of the course content.

If you are unable to see the correct courses on your home screen then please talk to your teacher.

Messaging

You will receive messages about your assignments through the message centre. Please check to see if you have any notification whenever you login into Kerboodle.

Exploring the features

Spend time familiarising yourself with your Kerboodle platform, exploring the different tabs within the course. If you need any help, click Help at the top right of your screen or browse the other sections of this website now.

Need technical advice?

For help with browsers and other technical queries, please visit our dedicated technical support pages.
