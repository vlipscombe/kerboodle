Student - Getting Started

Your details

To access the Kerboodle website you will need to have received your login details from your teacher/school administrator. These details will consist of a username, password and institution code. .

If you have misplaced or forgotten your details then please speak to your teacher. Oxford University Press are unable to give out details to students.

Logging in

When you first login to the website you will need to change your password.

Please change your password and if you are able to add an email address. If you have an email address registered on your account, you will be able to reset your password if you need to in the future. Please click on the Save Changes button after you have made any changes to your details.
