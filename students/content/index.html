Finding content

Every course is made up of different sections - yours may contain all of them, or just some of them.

This page will give you help for using sections called Lessons, Resources, or Practice. If you need help with using Assessment or Homework sections of your course, then visit the Completing and Submitting Work help pages.

If you need help using a Digital Book, then visit the Using the Digital Book.

Using sections called Lessons, Resources or Practice

In Lessons, Resources and Practice you can use content, and complete tests and activities, but your work will not be saved, and any marks will not go into your markbook.

So you can complete the activities as many times as you want.

If you are not sure what work to look at, ask your teacher.

Finding and opening content

To open an item click once on the title.

To find an item use the folder structure on the left-hand side. Clicking on a title in here will filter the main list of content to just the items within your chosen section.

You can also try using the Search bar at the top of your screen. Keep your search terms simple. You can click on Advanced if you want to search for a particular type of content in the Resources or Practice sections of the course.

To preview select an item from the list (using the checkbox to the left of the title) then click on Preview above the list.
