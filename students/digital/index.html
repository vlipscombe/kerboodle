Using the Digital Book

Every course is made up of different sections - yours may contain all of them, or just some of them.

This page will give you help for using the Digital Book section.

If you need help with using any other section of your course, then return to the Student help pages home.

Using the section called Digital Book

To open a book, click on the cover.

To move through the book, click on the forward and back buttons to the bottom left and right of the window, or enter a page number in the Go To Page field, bottom right.

To add notes to a page click on the Tools button.

Q: How do I open a book?

A: Click on the book cover. The book will open in a new tab or window, depending on your browser settings.

Q: What do all the buttons do?

A: This is an explanation of each of the buttons, from left to right along the Digital Book toolbar.



Backward and Forward - these will move you page by page through the book



Resources - this gives quick access to any additional resources that relate to this Digital Book



Notes - a place to write and save personal notes



Bookmarks - use this tool to create a quick link to particular pages



Whiteboard Tools - make use of a pen, highlighter, sticky notes and a spotlight tool. Save, and hide or reveal your annotations



Single page/Double page view - click on either of these to display just a single page at a time, or to display a spread at a time



Zoom in/Zoom out - click the plus to zoom in, the minus to zoom out. Once zoomed in, click on the book page and drag to reposition



Fullscreen - click on this to cancel the zoom



Table of Contents - clicking on a link within the table of contents will jump you to that point in the book



Go to Page - enter a number and hit return (or equivalent button) on your keyboard to jump to any page in the book



Backward and Forward - these will move you page by page through the book

Q: How do I turn the pages?

A: Click on the forward and back arrows to the bottom right, and bottom left, of the window.

Q: Can I draw on the book?

A: Yes - click on the Whiteboard Tools button at the bottom of the screen to open the tools menu. Choose a tool by clicking on it, then click on the page to start using it.

Click on Undo to remove your last annotation.

To delete something, select the Eraser then click on the thing you wish to remove.

Show a single part of the screen with the Spotlight tool. Select Spotlight, then click on the page and drag to reveal the desired area. Click again to remove the spotlight and reveal the whole page.

Click again on the Whiteboard Tools button to close the menu.

Q: Can I make notes?

A: Yes - click on the Notebook button {notebook icon} at the bottom of the screen to open the Notebook. Clicking on a notebook page allows you to enter and format text, among other things. To save your notes, click Save Changes at the bottom of the notebook.

Each page of the notebook refers to a specific page in the Digital Book, as indicated by the number in the bottom corner of each notebook page. Clicking on this number takes you to that Digital Book page.

Any notes you make will only be visible by yourself, but all notes can be exported as an text file (.rtf) file by clicking Export at the bottom of the notebook.

Q: Can I bookmark a page?

A: Yes you can. Go to the page in the digital book that you wish to bookmark and then click the Bookmark icon. Give a name to the bookmark and then press enter.

To delete a bookmark, click on the bookmark icon and then click on the cross icon to the left of the bookmark name. Alternatively, if you are already on the bookmarked page, you can click on the bookmark icon and then click Unbookmark this page.

Q: How do I get my cursor back after using the Whiteboard Tools?

A: Open the tools menu again, and select the Cursor.

Q: How do I zoom in and out?

A: Use the plus and minus buttons at the bottom of your screen, in the middle. Once zoomed, click and drag the page to change the area you are looking at.

Use the minus button to zoom back out, or the Fullscreen button to the right.

Q: Can I view a single page?

A: Yes. To the left of the plus (zoom) button is an option to switch between spread view and single page view. Click the option you want to switch the view.

Q: Can I show only a single part of the screen?

A: Yes - you can use the Zoom tools (see How do I zoom in and out?) or the Spotlight tool (see Can I make notes?).

Q: How do I skip to a certain page?

A: Click in the Go To Page box to the bottom right of your screen. Enter a page number, then hit Return or Go on your keyboard.

You can also make use of the Table of Contents to the left of the Go to Page box. Click the button to open the table, and click on the section that you would like to jump to.
