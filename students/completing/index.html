Completing and submitting work

Every course is made up of different sections - yours may contain all of them, or just some of them.

This page will give you help for using Assessment or Homework.

If you need help with using Lessons, Resources, or Practice sections of your course, then visit the Finding Content help pages.

If you need help using a Digital Book, then visit the Using the Digital Book help pages.

Using sections called Assessment or Homework

In Assessment and Homework you will only see content that your teacher has sent to you. You need to complete this work and hand it in.

If you are struggling, or are not sure what to do, ask your teacher.

Q: How do I find the work I need to do?

A:

In your Assessment or Homework section, you will only see work your teacher has sent to you.

To the top left of your screen are four filters to help you find your work:

  • Current Assignments - this will show you any work you need to complete
  • Due today - this will show you any work that needs to be handed in today
  • Due this week - this will show you any work that needs to be handed in this week
  • Expired (incomplete submission) - this will show you any work where you have missed your deadline. You might want to contact your teacher about anything in here.

Q: How do I open the work?

A:

Click on the title of the assignment you want to look at. The work you need to do will be in the top right corner of the box that opens - under the title: Test Material. Click on the work to open it. Look out for any special instructions from your teacher, which will show under Assignment Info.

If it is an interactive piece of work you need to complete it on-screen. You must click on Submit at the end of your work, to make sure your work gets marked.

If the assignment is not interactive, something like a Word file, PDF or PowerPoint will open. You need to follow the instructions and complete your work in a suitable way.

You can choose to upload the work online, or you may be able to give it in by hand.

Q: How do I hand in my work?

A:

If the work is interactive, it will automatically be handed in by the system.

Otherwise, you can upload your piece of work, or give it in by hand. Your teacher may have left particular instructions, so look out for them.

To submit your work online, you need to be in your Assessment or Homework section of the right course. Click on the title of the work, which will open the assignment window. To the bottom left is a box called Your Work, and in here is an Upload button. Click on this button, then choose and open your file. It should now be listed under Your Work.

You now have two options:

  • Save and Close will save your work, but it will NOT send it to your teacher. If you choose this option, you must go back at some point and click Submit, or you will miss your deadline.
  • Submit will save your work and send it to your teacher. You cannot make any changes to it after this point.

Q: What does ticking the ‘I am giving my work in by hand’ box do?

A: This option is only possible if you have been assigned a non-interactive piece of work, usually a Word document or a PDF. It allows your teacher to record a mark for the work in your Markbook, even though you have given your homework to your teacher in person. If your teacher has asked you to give in your work online, you shouldn’t tick this box. You will need to upload your work and then click Submit. For more help with this, see How do I hand in my work? above.

Q: I’ve made a mistake - can I change what I’ve given in?

A: No, once you have clicked Submit, you can’t change anything about your work. If you have uploaded the wrong file or ticked the ‘I am giving my work in my hand’ box by mistake, please speak to your teacher for help.

Q: What if I miss my deadline?

A:

If you miss your deadline you will no longer be able to submit your work online.

Talk to your teacher if you are not sure what to do.

Q: How do I find out my marks?

A:

To see work you have handed in, you need to go into your Markbook - click on the Markbook tab when you’re in your course.

In here, there are three filters to the top left of your screen:

  • Recently marked - this will show you any work that has been marked during this calendar (Monday to Sunday) week
  • All assignments - this will show you all the work you have done in the course
  • Awaiting marks - this will show any work that you are still waiting to receive marks for

You can also use the Search bar, if you can remember the name of the assignment.

Click on the title of the assignment in the list. This will open up a window.

Your mark for this piece of work will be displayed in the bottom right, in the Mark box.

Q: How do I see the work I handed in?

A:

To see work you have handed in, you need to go into your Markbook - click on the Markbook tab when you’re in your course.

In here, there are three filters to the top left of your screen:

  • Recently marked - this will show you any work that has been marked during this calendar (Monday to Sunday) week
  • All assignments - this will show you all the work you have done in the course
  • Awaiting marks - this will show any work that you are still waiting to receive marks for

You can also use the Search bar, if you can remember the name of the assignment.

Click on the title of the assignment in the list. This will open up a window.

If your work was interactive, then you can click through the screens in the bottom right.

If your work was not interactive, it will be in the bottom left of this window. Click on it to open.
