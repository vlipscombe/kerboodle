Login

Welcome to the Online support technical support help page. Use this page to find the answers to many of the most common issues encountered by our users when they are trying to login into our online products

To learn about the student and teacher experiences visit the Student support pages and Teacher Support pages

If you cannot resolve your query, please Contact us.

Technical Frequently asked questions

Students

Q: My login is not working?

A: Check that you have the correct username, password and institution code. If you are unsure of any of these details then please ask your teacher for your details. Unfortunately we cannot give out password to parents or students.

Q: I have forgotten my institution code?

A:

Check with your teacher or friends to find out the institution code for your school. The code should contain two letters and one number.

For example -

Institution code - jh4

Q: I have forgotten my username?

A:

Check with your teacher to find out your username details. The standard format for a username is the first letter of your first name and your full surname.

For example

name - Paul Smith
username - psmith

Teachers

Q: I have forgotten my login details

A: If you have forgotten your username, password or institution code you can be sent these detail again by using the “Forgot my details” button in the top right hand corner of the login screen login screen. Click the link and a window will appear asking for your email address that you used to setup your online account. Once you click “Reset password” then an email with be sent out to your account. Please check your inbox and junk mail folders for the “Reset password instructions” email

Q: My student has forgotten their password - what can I do?

A: You can reset their password - this will return it to the default, which is the same as their username. Find the student in the User Management module, using the Search bar, or the Groups filters on the left. Select the student from the list, and click on Preview from above the list. This will open the Preview pane. In it there is an option Reset Password. Click on the tickbox, then click Save Changes at the bottom. The student’s password is now reset.

Q: Do you have a poster to remind students of the Institution code?

A: Yes, please download the Institution Code Poster and print it for your classrooms.

Q: How do I add students?

A: Only the Administrator can add students - this ensures that students only receive one set of login details if OxBox Online is used by multiple departments.

If your institution already has an Administrator, you can discover who they are by clicking on your name at the very top of the screen. Their details will be shown at the bottom of the My Profile window that opens.

If there is not already an Administrator in place you will be asked to invite one each time you enter the User Management module. Entering their details generates emails asking them to activate their account, and explaining all about the system and how to add students. If you want to find out more information please visit the Teacher User Management help page.
