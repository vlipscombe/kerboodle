Accessing the Kerboodle Courses and Books

In order to view the Kerboodle Courses and Books, your teacher needs to assign them to you.

If you can't see any courses or books on your homepage, it might be that your teacher hasn't assigned a course to you yet.

What is a Group Code?

When your Teacher assigns the course to your Teaching Group, they might give you a Group Code to use.

The Group Code allows you to add yourself to the Teaching Group and this will assign the course to you.

How do I use the Group Code?

To join a Teaching Group using a Group Code, click on the Join a Teaching Group button, enter the Group Code and click Add to group.
