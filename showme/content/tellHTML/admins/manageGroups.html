What are Teaching Groups and why are they needed?

When students are set up on Kerboodle, they are not given access to a Kerboodle Book or Course automatically; they need to be assigned.

Teaching Groups are what Kerboodle uses to determine which books and courses each student is able to access. Students can be in more than one Teaching Group so they need just one login for all of their subjects.

You can use the Teaching Groups to really personalise your students' learning - you could create new Teaching Groups for different ability bandings, after school classes or subject cohorts. You can create as many groups as you wish.

Creating Teaching Groups

How? To create a Teaching Group, access the Teaching Groups area and click on the Create a New Teaching Group button.

Prefer to see things in action? Have a look at our interactive step-by-step guides: Creating a Teaching Group

What is Group Code?

A Group Code option appears as an additional field available to you when creating or editing your Teaching Groups.

Using Group Code is really handy as it reduces the amount of time you have to spend setting up the Teaching Group. You only have to add the Courses and Books the students need access to and don't need to add any students at all. Simply tell your students what the code is and let them attach themselves to the group!

Updating and Editing Teaching Groups

If a new student needs to be added to a Teaching Group, or the group require access to an additional course or book, you can update the Teaching Group on-screen.

How? In the Teaching Groups area, click on the Teaching Group you would like to edit and then click on the drop down arrow on the right hand side of the screen. You then have the option to:

  • Edit this Teaching Group - this is useful for changing the name or description of the group.
  • Delete this Teaching Group
  • Add Student or a Teacher to this Teaching Group - use this otpion to add any new or extra students as needed.
  • Add a Course to this Teaching Group - use this option to add extra Courses or Kerboodle Books to the group.
