Viewing the Kerboodle resources

When you log into Kerboodle, all of the courses your Organisation has subscribed to (or are evaluating) will be available to you.

The Nelson Thornes content is contained within the course library, and where appropriate, it will follow the structure of the accompanying student book.

How? To view the library resources, click on the course title on the homepage. The course will open automatically on the library and you can use the + icons to open the tree structure and view the units and chapters. Then, click on the resource titles to view the activities and work available.

Searching for resources

To easily find content that relates to the topic you're teaching, use the handy Search feature in the Library.

How? To search in the course, enter your search term into the Search field on the Library bar, and click on the arrow. Your Search Results will then appear below under a new 'Search Results' bar.

Kerboodle Books

Kerboodle Books are interactive versions of the accompaning student book. You can add annotations, notes, links and highlight important text.

How? If your school has purchased Kerboodle Books, they can be accessed through Kerboodle in the following ways:

  • Clicking on the Book title on your homepage - this will take you straight to your book
  • Clicking on the Go to my Bookshelf button - this will launch a carousel or grid view of all of your Kerboodle Books
  • Clicking Launch Kerboodle Book from the course page - this will take you straight to your book.

Uploading your own resources

As well as the Nelson Thornes content that is available on Kerboodle, you can also add your own resources so that all your teaching material is in one place.

These resources can then be assigned to students, combined with the existing resources when creating lessons and can also be included in the library by editing the course.

How? To upload your own resources, access your course and click on the My Resources bar. Then click on the Upload a Resource button to browse for your file and upload it.
