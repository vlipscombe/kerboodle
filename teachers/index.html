Frequently asked questions

Q: I’ve logged in – what now?

A: The first page you see is your homepage – the courses you have access to will be shown in tiles in the centre of your screen. Click on a course tile to gain access to all the content.

Q: I can’t see the course I purchased/I need to access – what should I do?

A:

Each product you buy will belong to a course. Find your product by clicking on the appropriate course tile in the centre of your screen. If you have access to more than six courses, use the forward and back arrows to scroll.

If you can’t see the course(s) you want or expect, contact your Administrator. To find out who your Administrator is click on My Profile. To find this, look to the top of your screen, and click on your name. Scroll to the bottom of the window that opens, and your school’s Administrator will be displayed.

If you do not yet have an allocated Administrator, and you cannot see or access your course, please contact us.

Q: What are the recommendations for?

A:

The ‘Also recommended’ panel will show you other products that might be of interest to you and your department based on the courses you already have access to. Clicking on one of these recommendations will give you the option of visiting our website for more details. The panel is only there to provide you with helpful information – it is not possible to purchase directly from Kerboodle, and this panel is not visible to your students.

Q: Can I change my login details?

A:

Yes you can. At the top of your screen you will see your name is displayed. Click on your name, and a window will open containing all of your details. Simply type in new information, and click Save Changes.

Q: How do I create logins for my colleagues and students?

A:

Visit our User Management support pages for full information on setting up other users.

Q: What information does my Systems Administrator/Data Manager need?

A:

Your Data Manager can access all the information they need from the Adninstrator support pages.

Q: What do my students see when they login?

A:

Your students will have a very similar experience to you – although some things are not visible to them, such as the ‘Also recommended’ panel. If you would like to find out about the student experience, click here to visit their dedicated support pages.

Q: Can I use this with my VLE?

A:

You can create links in Kerboodle to save in your VLE. These links will point to a specific location, not to items of content.

When working in your VLE, you can then click on this link to open Kerboodle. If you are not already logged into Kerboodle then you will be prompted to login to gain access.

To create a link, go to the course, section (Resources, Lessons, Homework, Practice, or Assessment) and folder (using the left-hand folder structure) of Kerboodle that you would like to create a link to. Then click on the More button above the main list. Choose Copy Link to VLE. Click on the link to select it, then use Ctrl + C, or Cmd + C to copy. You can then paste this link into your VLE in the usual way.

Students will be able to use these links from your VLE as well, but a link to the Assessment or Homework section will not work for them - as content here has to be assigned first.

Q: I have invited a teacher but they still haven’t received their email.

A:

Please make sure that you are typing in the right email address when inviting the teacher. We get a surprising amount of email bounce back due to the wrong address being typed in.

Q: Who should purchase online products for my institution?

A:

Ideally, teachers should purchase products to be used within their department, but where this isn’t possible, the Administrator should purchase products using a different email address to that which has been used to set up their Administrator account. Please note that doing this will create a new Teacher account.
