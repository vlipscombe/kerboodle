View the Library resources

The Kerboodle resources are stored in the course Library. The course structure will be similar to that of your student book, so you can easily move through the course while following the book.

How? For step-by-step instructions, please have a look at our Show Me How tutorial: Show Me How!

To easily find content that relates to the topic you're studying, use the handy Search feature in the Library.

How? For step-by-step instructions, please have a look at our Show Me How tutorial: Show Me How!

Access the Kerboodle Books

If your teacher has assigned you Kerboodle Books, you can access these by clicking on the book title on your homepage, or clicking on the Go to my Bookshelf or Launch Kerboodle Book button on the course page.

How? For step-by-step instructions, please have a look at our Show Me How tutorial: Show Me How!

Upload your own resources

As well as the Nelson Thornes content that is available on Kerboodle, you can also add your own resources so that all your studying material is in one place.

Click on the My Resources bar and use the Upload a resource button to browse and find the file you want to upload.

How? For step-by-step instructions, please have a look at our Show Me How tutorial: Show Me How!
