Top 4 Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Help with logging in
    • Each user requires a username, password and institution code.
    • See the Logging in support pages for information to support each type of user (teacher, student, administrator).
    • Unfortunately we (Oxford) are unable to give student login details to students, or their parents. Only their school can do this.

  2. Missing emails
    • When a new customer purchases a course they will receive two emails from OxBox - one of which is crucial as it allows them to activate their account.
    • If they do not receive these emails, they should first check their Spam/Junk email folder.
    • If they cannot find them, then their details should be passed onto 2nd line Technical support who can regenerate the email. Please make sure that the email address edtechsupport@oup.com has been added to their whitelist/safe senders list. If they are unsure of how to do this then please get them to contact their IT department.
    • See the Logging in support pages for more information.

  3. Browsers
    • There are some browsers in which the online product will not work correctly - the most likely problem will be with Internet Explorer 8. The customer can try using an alternative browser, or look into upgrading the version they have. See the Browsers support page for further information. About 80% of our customers are likely to use an appropriate browser - of the remaining 20%, the majority are likely to have an alternative they can use.

  4. Administrator - setting up
    • Many departments in the same school may use the online product courses, so an Administrator is required in each school to ensure that each user only has one set of login details. Someone in the IT department, or the Data Manager, would be appropriate. See the Administrator support pages for full information about this role.
